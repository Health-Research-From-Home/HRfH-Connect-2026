## Introduction

**HRfH Connect** took place at **Google HQ in London on 13 February 2026**. This inaugural event brought together a community of people involved in creating world-leading health research using smartphones and wearables. These attendees included academics, industry representatives, and members of the public.

> To read more about this event and to sign up to the waiting list for the next HRfH Connect, please visit our website:  
> **[HRfH Connect Annual Event](https://sites.manchester.ac.uk/health-research-from-home/2024/09/12/annual-event/)**
